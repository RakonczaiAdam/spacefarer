### Wormhole Navigation Levels

1. Drift Runner
Moves through safe space lanes, avoiding basic debris fields.

2. Route Keeper
Maintains stable trajectories along pre-mapped corridors.

3. Vector Pilot
Actively adjusts flight paths against minor gravitational drift.

4. Hazard Skipper
Specialist in bypassing asteroid clusters and weak spatial distortions.

5. Corridor Navigator
Manages mid-risk worm lanes with consistent turbulence and interference.

6. Rift Handler
Responds to sudden spatial anomalies, folds, and gravity shear events.

7. Deep Transit Navigator
Operates in unstable or partially collapsing worm routes.

8. Multi-Gate Synchronizer
Coordinates linked wormholes, maintaining alignment across multiple gates while avoiding phase desync and route collapse.

9. Singularity Skipper
Navigates extreme environments near black holes, temporal eddies, and high-risk fold boundaries.

10. Nexus Pathmaster
Elite role capable of full wormspace traversal control, dynamically rerouting entire fleets through unstable or evolving gate networks.
